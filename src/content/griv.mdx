## Griv

Griv is a social fitness app where your camera is your gym buddy.

**Snap a photo** → get instant **AI feedback** → earn **points** → **climb** the leaderboard.

Whether you’re lifting, running, cycling, boxing, hiking, or just eating clean. **Griv captures it**, scores it, and **makes it social** on Farcaster & Base app.

### Overview

#### Features

- AI-powered instant feedback on fitness activities
- Points and leaderboard system for motivation
- Social sharing on Farcaster & Base app
- Supports every health activity

#### User Experience

- Snap or upload a photo to log your activity
- Receive instant AI feedback and score
- Earn points and climb the leaderboard
- Share achievements and progress with friends
- Connect and compete with the fitness community

Griv makes fitness fun, social, and rewarding—turning your camera into your gym buddy.
