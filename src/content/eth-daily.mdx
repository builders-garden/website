## Ethereum Daily

A mini app where users read daily articles and take quizzes to earn rewards based on their performance.

### How Rewards Work
Rewards come from auctions where sponsors can bid to get the spotlight for the next quiz, creating a sustainable reward pool for participants.

### Admin Panel

- Easily manage auctions and add new posts
- Auto-generate AI-powered quizzes from articles
- Monitor user engagement and performance

### User Experience

- Read daily articles and check available rewards
- Take quizzes based on the content
- Earn rewards based on correct answers
- Build daily streaks that boost reward amounts

### Auction System

- View current and past auctions
- Browse active bidding for quiz sponsorship spots
- Place bids to sponsor the next quiz rewards
