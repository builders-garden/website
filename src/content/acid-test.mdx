## Acid Test
An **onchain radio show** bringing music, conversation, and creative energy to Farcaster. Hosted by [chaim.eth](https://farcaster.xyz/chaim.eth).

**Acid Test brings** together music and community, letting users collect and share unique onchain audio experiences.

### Admin Features

- **Upload** new episodes with audio and metadata
- Send manual **notifications to users**

### User Experience

- **Browse** and **listen** to all Acid Test episodes
- **Mint episode NFTs** (ERC1155) directly from the app
- **Share minted** episodes on Farcaster
- View episode **details**, **stats**, **collectors** and more
