## UFO

UFO is the open internet radio. A radio network broadcasting and archiving live shows, mixes, conversations, and sound experiments from artists, DJs, and scenes around the world. Free to tune into, open to contribute to.

### Features

- Multiple channels hosting podcasts, radio mixes, and more
- Live streaming of shows and events
- Archive of past broadcasts and episodes
- Open platform for artists and communities to contribute

### User Experience

- Browse and listen to live and archived shows
- Explore different channels and genres
- Access podcasts, mixes, and sound experiments
- Join live streams from anywhere
